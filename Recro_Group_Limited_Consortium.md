# Recro Group Limited-Consortium

## Description

Recro Group Limited is a behavioral health organization that provides psychotherapy and training in the Nairobi area. As part of enhancing best practice, Recro Group has developed a behavioral health consortium. The Consortium brings together a diverse group of like-minded behavioral health professionals (mental and medical health) for the purposes of:

a) Sharpening clinical and research skills
b) Providing ethically sound services
c) Collaboration with the other professionals who uphold the high level of professionalism and who want to maintain best practices in the field

## Mission

The consortium strives to provide a forum for behavioral health professionals with collective and unified voices to grow in profession and have support from one another. By promoting the dissemination and equitable progression of practices, (as a member) you benefit from the sharing of resources, networking, increasing skillsets, and collaborating with the other professionals.

## Members

**Who the consortium is for:**

Professionals who have a Masters or Doctorate degree in Marriage and Family Therapy, Medicine, Allied health, Clinical Psychology and Counseling Psychology from accredited Universities.

## Requirements

- Adherence to your ethical and legal guidelines as stipulated by your profession
- Attendance of the monthly meetings (2nd Thursday of every month at 7:30–9:30am)
- Participation in the meetings
- Have the character and zeal to meet the best practices; clinically and in research as per your professional areas of interest
- Must be a member of your professional governing body (Example: Psychologists must be a member of the Counsellors and Psychologists Board) (CPB, AAMFT, APA, KMA etc.)
